Meet Team Ladybug

We are a passionate group of student researchers from the Monash RED unit, working on ways to end Technology-Facilitated Gender-Based Violence (TFGBV) in the Pacific. Our team brings together diverse perspectives and shared purpose to create safer digital futures for women and girls.

Moe

Cyber Security Specialist. Focused on digital safety education and youth outreach through media and workshops.

Maria

Passionate about community-based intervention and culturally responsive policy development.

Sabreeen

Investigates the role of emerging technologies like AI in enabling or combating TFGBV.

Yoyo

Leads workshop designs and outreach efforts across Pacific youth communities.
